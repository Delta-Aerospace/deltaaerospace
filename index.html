About Us

Delta Aerospace Systems is focused on cutting-edge aerospace development — from flight computers to deployment systems and 3D-printed hardware. Our work is open-source and community-driven.

Projects & Technology

Flight computer designed for servo rocket deployment, telemetry, and GPS tracking — built around ESP32-C3 and sensors.

Moses-Hank Framework

Flight computer framework for rapid development of rocket avionics and mission control systems.

Parachute 3D Models

CAD and 3D design files for rocket parachutes and recovery hardware — optimized for reliability and ease of use.
